title: 'Download audio from Youtube videos on Mac'

date: 2022-3-1 10:19:00 +1100
categories: Mac Terminal
---

1. [Install Homebrew](https://brew.sh/)
2. Install Youtube-dl `brew install youtube-dl`
3. Replace Youtube URL `youtube-dl -f 140 'https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-44VdVFU9pY'`
